Ingredients How to Use

oat, puffed rice, sunflower seeds, dried cranberries, dried apricot, apple sauce or maple syrup, vanilla, cinnamon, sea salt

  • Sweet use: as a quick nutritious snack to go or serve it on yogurt or chopped fruit as a breakfast or dessert.
  • Savoury use: sprinkle on top of salad for a touch of sweet crunch, our current favourite is on top of an arugula and feta cheese salad.
