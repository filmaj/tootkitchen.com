Kimchi

A Korean classic, spicy, fizzy fermented vegetable medley.

Comes in Regular and Spicy flavours.

$5 for 200 g (237 ml), $8.50 for 400 g (475 ml)

Use to flavour stir fries

Makes an excellent side dish

Great with fried eggs

Add to stews for extra kick

Ingredients How to Use

nappa cabbage, carrot, daikon, green onion, ginger, Asian pear, garlic Korean red pepper and fish sauce

As a side dish or in stir fries, stews, soups or salads. Mix in flour and cornstarch and fry to make kimchi pancakes. Our current favorite is adding a couple of spoons of this kimchi to fried eggs or on top of poached eggs. High in fiber and probiotics.
