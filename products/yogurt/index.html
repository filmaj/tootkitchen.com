Yogurt & Labneh

Classic yogurt

Hand made, small batch, creamy and tangy. Available in both plain and flavoured.

Ingredients: Milk, probiotic cultures.

How to use:

  • For savory use: serve it as a side dish to complement mains, a healthier alternative to sour cream or mayonnaise, on soups or in tuna or potato salad. Use a snack accompaniment with bagels, bread or potato chips (our favorite and an Iranian party classic). Suggested recipe: mix in chopped aromatic herbs, pressed garlic and diced cucumber for a classic Persian Mast-o-Khiar.
  • For sweet use: serve with granola or fruit salad for a protein, probiotic-rich breakfast or dessert. Use in baking for a healthier, more nutritious option or as an alternative to buttermilk.

Thick labneh

Rich, thick, strained yogurt. Available in both plain and flavoured.

Ingredients: Milk, probiotic cultures, double-strained.

How to use:

  • For savory use: serve it as an appetizer with chopped carrots, celery, apple, flat bread or chips. Spread it on your favorite bread with chopped herbs as a sandwich base.
  • For sweet use: sprinkle granola on top with a side of chopped fruits. Spread on your favorite bread with pieces of nuts and dried fruits. Our favorite is labneh on bagels with walnut and raisins.
