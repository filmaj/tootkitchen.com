  • For savory use: serve it as a side dish to complement mains, a healthier alternative to sour cream or mayonnaise, on soups or in tuna or potato salad. Suggested recipe: mix in chopped aromatic herbs, pressed garlic and diced cucumber for a classic Persian Mast-o-Khiar.
  • For sweet use: serve with granola or fruit salad for a protein, probiotic-rich breakfast or dessert. Use in baking for a healthier, more nutritious option or as an alternative to buttermilk.
