Kombucha

Fizzy probiotic drink, flavoured with fruits, dried herbs, flowers and spices.

Ingredients: organic black tea and brown sugar (culture food).

How to use: On its own as a refreshing probiotic drink or mix in with your favourite liquor. Our current favourites are mango kombucha with tequila and cardamom kombucha with whisky.
