Pickled Turnip

Sour, crispy, preserved turnip and beets with Middle Eastern spices.

Ingredients: beets, turnip, garlic, dried mint, dried taragon, garlic, black pepper, apple cider vinegar

How to use: A Middle Eastern classic, use in sandwiches or wraps or chopped up in tuna or potato salad for an extra kick of flavor.
